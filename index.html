CLASS OF 2024

혼돈으로 만들어지고, 사랑으로 이어진다.

In our classroom, time didn’t just pass — it lingered, gently, like sunlight on a quiet morning. We were twenty different stories, twenty different dreams, yet somehow, when we sat together, we became one chapter. Some of us laughed loudly, some spoke softly, and some communicated only through sleepy eyes and late-night snacks. But even in our differences, we found warmth — a kind of warmth that only exists in places where people truly care.

There were days filled with noise and chaos, days when we argued over the smallest things, and days when we stayed up late finishing projects we swore we’d start earlier. But looking back, every moment feels precious, like scenes from a youth drama we didn’t even know we were filming.

Our class was never perfect, but it was real — beautifully, honestly real. It was a place where friendships grew slowly and naturally, like spring flowers blooming a little more each day.

This yearbook is not just a record of a school year; it’s a reminder of the laughter that echoed in our room, the secrets whispered during breaks, and the memories that will quietly follow us long after we leave these halls. Here’s to us — a class that became a small universe of our own.
